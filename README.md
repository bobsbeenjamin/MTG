## MTG
An online way to build and test sealed decks, built in good ol' HTML/JavaScript.

I let this project go stale when in-person Magic died for 2 years because of COVID, but this was how I prepared myself for new sets for many years.

To see the working part in action:
 - Go to https://bobsbeenjamin.github.io/MTG/MTG_SealedDeck.html
 - Select the set you want to test at the bottom
 - Sort and group the cards in the top section (your card pool)
 - Click individual cards in your card pool to add them to your deck, and click cards in your deck to put them back in your pool
 - Add basics when you're ready to test
 - Click Try Deck to shuffle up and draw a sample hand
 - Do all this a few times to get ready for playing sealed deck for your favorite set!

**Note:** The main landing page points to sub-projects that are works in progress
